The SEP Checker

Instructions:

Use the drop-down menu to check if the SEP is Compatible or Not Compatible with the firmware you wish to restore to.

If the SEP is compatible, you can restore your device to that firmware with valid blobs.

If the SEP is not compatible, then you cannot restore to that firmware.

The currently signed firmwares for tvOS are: 13.4.8, 13.4.6 and 10.2.2
